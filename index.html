Research

University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign

Graduate Research Assistant

Guide : Dr. Klara Nahrstedt

I have been working on developing an action recognition and detection system for 360-degree firefighting videos. To achieve this, I have compared the performance of different algorithms, including CNN, CNN-LSTM, CNN-FPN, RNN, and 3D bag of words, for action detection. The average detection accuracy of the developed system currently stands at 89%.

Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi (IIT)

Undergraduate Research Intern

Guide : Dr. Saif Khan Mohammed

I worked on modeling an unmanned aerial vehicle with a LIDAR sensor for object detection using MATLAB and Simulink. The system was capable of autonomously rerouting its course by self-plotting new waypoints and identifying obstructions, achieving an average detection accuracy of 88.21%.

Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani

Undergraduate Research Intern

Guide : Dr. Vinay Chamola

I delved into the study of public key post-quantum cryptosystems. I explored various cryptosystems, including code-based, lattice-based, multivariate-based, supersingular elliptic curve isogeny cryptosystems, and hybrid cryptosystems, gaining a comprehensive understanding of their working principles. Understanding different cryptosystems is crucial for working with cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology. It enabled me to evaluate cryptographic mechanisms in blockchain protocols and contribute to the design of secure consensus algorithms.
